# Programming the HDK

## Via the ICSP connectors

Plug the Connections in to the header as follows:

| Port | Connection | Port | Connection |
|:----:|:----------:|:----:|:----------:|
| 1    |  MISO      |  4   |   VCC (5V) |
| 2    |    SCK     |  5   |    MOSI    |
| 3    |  RESET     |  6   |   GND      |
